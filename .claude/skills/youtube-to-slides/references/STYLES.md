# Style Presets

youtube-to-slides supports 4 visual styles for infographic generation. Each produces a distinctly different aesthetic.

## davinci (default)

**Renaissance-era scientific manuscript**

Hand-drawn ink illustrations on aged parchment, reminiscent of Leonardo da Vinci's notebooks. Features anatomical diagrams, cross-hatching, and ornate calligraphy.

- **Colors:** Aged yellowed parchment, dark brown and sepia ink, occasional red-brown accents
- **Layout:** Asymmetric hand-drawn layout with ornate calligraphy and scattered annotations
- **Visuals:** Detailed pen-and-ink sketches â€” gears, pulleys, botanical cross-sections, geometric constructions

**Best for:** Educational content, science/engineering topics, history, thoughtful analysis pieces.

---

## magazine

**High-end editorial spread**

Black and white with dramatic contrast, inspired by Monocle-style Swiss typography. Razor-thin rules, generous negative space, and bold typographic hierarchy.

- **Colors:** Pure black and white only, no gray tones except subtle halftone textures
- **Layout:** Grid-based editorial layout, oversized bold headline, two-column or single-column, pull-quote style, thin black rules
- **Visuals:** Abstract geometric shapes (circles, lines, rectangles), no illustrations, high-contrast silhouettes or halftone patterns

**Best for:** Business topics, interviews, professional content, minimalist aesthetic.

---

## comic

**Vibrant comic book / pop art style**

Bold black outlines, Ben-Day dots, starburst shapes, speech bubbles, and dynamic diagonal compositions. High energy and visually striking.

- **Colors:** Bright saturated primaries (red, blue, yellow, green), thick black outlines, white speech bubbles, Ben-Day dot patterns
- **Layout:** Comic panel layout with asymmetric panels, explosive starburst headline, speech bubbles for key points, narrator box at bottom
- **Visuals:** Cartoon characters, comic onomatopoeia (POW, ZAP, BOOM), action lines, comic-style icons

**Best for:** Entertainment, tutorials, fun/casual topics, content aimed at younger audiences.

---

## geek

**College bulletin board / dorm room aesthetic**

Kraft paper and corkboard background with a mix of handwritten marker text, typed index cards, photos, sticky notes, stickers, and doodles. Deliberately messy and personal.

- **Colors:** Warm cork/kraft paper base, neon sticky notes (pink, yellow, green, blue), red/blue marker, black Sharpie, highlighter yellow
- **Layout:** Deliberately messy collage, black marker headline at top center, key points on sticky notes/index cards, red string connections, torn notebook page at bottom
- **Visuals:** Hand-drawn doodles (stick figures, flowcharts, arrows, stars, lightbulbs, coffee rings), printouts taped on, handwritten notes, pencil on board edge

**Best for:** Study notes, brainstorming summaries, creative topics, personal/informal content.
